Amboseli National Park

Amboseli National Park – The Land of Giants

Discover Africa’s Majestic Beauty with Rumaa Tours

Amboseli National Park, nestled at the foot of Mount Kilimanjaro, is one of Kenya’s most iconic safari destinations, renowned for its vast elephant herds, stunning landscapes, and unparalleled views of Africa’s highest peak. This compact yet vibrant park is a haven for wildlife, hosting four of the Big Five (lion, elephant, leopard, and buffalo) alongside cheetahs, giraffes, and diverse birdlife. The park’s swamps and savannahs create a dramatic backdrop for unforgettable game drives and cultural encounters with the Maasai people.

Ideal for families, photographers, and nature lovers, Amboseli offers intimate wildlife experiences, luxurious lodges, and the chance to witness Kilimanjaro’s snow-capped summit at sunrise.

Why Visit Amboseli National Park

  • Observe large elephant herds up close in their natural habitat

  • Enjoy breathtaking views of Mount Kilimanjaro, Africa’s tallest mountain

  • Spot four of the Big Five and a variety of other wildlife

  • Experience year-round game viewing in diverse ecosystems

  • Capture iconic wildlife photography with Kilimanjaro as a backdrop

  • Take a scenic hot air balloon ride over the park’s wetlands

  • Witness vibrant birdlife, including flamingos and pelicans

  • Connect with Maasai culture through authentic village visits

  • Stay in eco-friendly lodges or intimate tented camps

  • Explore a UNESCO Biosphere Reserve celebrated for conservation
