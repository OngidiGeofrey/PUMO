Explore Amazing Nairobi National Park – Half-Day Safari Experience

Located just 7 kms from Nairobi’s city centre, Nairobi National Park is the perfect place for a half-day or full-day excursion from the Kenyan capital. One of the only places on earth where you can be on safari with skyscrapers as part of your backdrop. A perfect option for those limited on time or wanting to combine their visit with a trip to one of Kenya’s other attractions. Keep your eyes peeled in search of lion, leopard, buffalo, rhino, jackal, and many other animals. Sunrise Hot Air Balloon in Maasai Mara National Park

A truly ‘once in a lifetime’ experience, flying in a Hot Air Balloon over the Masai Mara plains is an unforgettable experience that you have the chance to enjoy during any safari you take to Maasai Mara. The balloon take offs at the break of dawn, gliding silently during the ride over the savannah grasslands below where you may spot a variety of wildlife. The flight lasts an hour and concludes with an hour ”Champagne Bush Breakfast” at the balloon landing site in the wilderness. The balloon is flown by a fully qualified and experienced Pilot. Day Tour To Giraffe Center and Elephant Orphanage Project

Immerse yourself in a heartwarming Nairobi adventure with Pavillion Safaris’ Day Tour to the Giraffe Center and Elephant Orphanage Project! This half-day journey offers a rare chance to feed the endangered Rothschild giraffes up close at the Giraffe Centre, learning about their conservation story.
